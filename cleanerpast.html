STEM Camp
December 2023

Keynote Speaker: Dr. Andrew Zentner, PhD

Location: Pittsburgh, PA

For our in-person STEM Class, we taught a comprehensive curriculum covering the basics of olympiad/competitive Biology, Chemistry, and Physics. On the final day, we had an olympiad-style mock competition for the students to show off their skills, as well as a team buzzer playoff to mimic National Science Bowl.

International Chess Camp
December 2023

Location: Virtual

To accommodate a wider audience, we moved our chess camp to virtual and had students from all over the world join. We taught intermediate-level tactics, opening, middlegame, endgame, and meta-skills (psychology, game analysis, time managements) for our students to learn the content.

Spanish Chess Camp
December 2023

Location: Virtual


Recognizing the challenge of language barriers limiting access to problem-solving skills, we decided to create a Spanish division to Chesspire to further our mission across various languages, starting with Spanish. Our camp was a great success and included international players!
