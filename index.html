General Information

What is Chesspire?

Chesspire is a free chess training program for kids in the Greater Pittsburgh hosted by professional high schoolers. We provide a fun and engaging environment for kids to learn chess and improve their skills. We also give out prizes to the top competitors at each tournament (hosted weekly) and provide a free lunch for all participants.


This summer, we're hosting a training camp with two divisions, the Cubs and the Tigers. The cubs are for introductory chess players, where they will learn how to play the game and basic tactics. The tigers are for more advanced chess players, where you will learn more theory, advanced plays, and gambits.

Where is the camp?

We will be hosting our training camp at the North Allegheny Intermediate High School in rooms 210 and 311 *tentative*


As we move on with our program, we'll be doing online meetings through Zoom, but more information on that after the summer camp!
